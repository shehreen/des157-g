Paper Prototype

This paper prototype shows the multiple screens and features on the site. The first panel on the top left shows the main homescreen, where content, styled as different colored text bubbles, indicating the type of content will float across the screen indicated by the upward arrows. the plus sign is to add a post to the feed yourself. Clicking on one of the bubbles will take you to the next screen, which is a popup overlay.

The text bubble enlarges to show the whole content, which could contain a link to a youtube guided meditation, a song, or just some writing for how to be more mindful, life advice, or something positive to make your day better. It shows the posters name, as well as icons to share on various social media platform.

The third panel shows the login page, for when people want to add a post themselves or check out their collections. Option to login with facebook to see your friends on the app.

Fourth panel shows the options when creating your own post, and to add style, which is simple changing color or shape of the text bubble, and then share to add it to feed.

Clicking in the hamburger menu shows your username, and option to logout, or check out your saved posts.

The last panel shows your saved collections to scroll through and recharge your heart for the day.
